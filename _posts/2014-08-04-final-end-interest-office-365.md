Four months ago I moved from Google Apps to Office 365. I like Outlook and the other Office applications and thought they might be a good alternative to Google. There are quite a few minor quirks I was willing to dismiss, but below are the two halves of the final straw for me.

Local application error when trying to send email:![The operation failed. The messaging interfaces have returned an unknown error. If the problem persists, restart Outlook.]({{ site.url }}/images/2014/2014-08-04-final-end-interest-office-365/messaging-interfaces-returned-unknown-error.png)

Server based application error when I attempted to log in via the web client:
![Something went wrong. Uncaught reference error.]({{ site.url }}/images/2014/2014-08-04-final-end-interest-office-365/something-went-wrong.png)

The recommended resolution in the Microsoft help forums to the client error was to uninstall Outlook, delete the storage file, re-install, and reconfigure. I&#8217;m sorry, but I have higher value ways to spend my time.
