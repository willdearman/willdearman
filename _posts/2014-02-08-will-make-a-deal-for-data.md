Data continues to play an increasing role in corporate strategy. Cases in point, in the past two days there have been two high profile stories about established companies paying real money to ‘partner’ for data:

- [WSJ - Yahoo to Partner With Yelp on Local Search][http://online.wsj.com/news/articles/SB10001424052702304680904579371263386333816]
- [NYT - Foursquare Checks In With Microsoft and Gets $15 Million to Revitalize Itself][http://bits.blogs.nytimes.com/2014/02/04/foursquare-checks-in-with-microsoft-and-gets-15-million-to-revitalize-itself/?_php=true&_type=blogs&_r=0]

A few years from now I’m looking forward to reading about data being the primary driver in a few large M&A transactions.
